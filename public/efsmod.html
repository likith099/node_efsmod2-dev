Resource & Referral

Florida's Child Care Resource and Referral Network helps families locate quality child care and early education programs. Trained specialists offer information about local options and create customized lists based on your needs. Financial assistance strategies and referrals to other community resources are also available.

Learn More About CCR&R

School Readiness

Florida's School Readiness Program provides financial assistance to eligible families for early education and care. Services vary based on individual needs and can include extended day, after-school, and school-age care.

Apply for School Readiness

Voluntary Prekindergarten (VPK)

Florida's free VPK program prepares 4-year-olds for kindergarten and beyond. Families can choose school-year or summer programs offered by private centers and public schools.
