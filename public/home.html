Welcome to the EFSMOD Portal. This portal provides access to essential services and information for families in Florida. You can apply for various programs and services online by clicking on the Apply for buttons below.

Resource & Referral
The EFSMOD Resource and Referral Network helps families locate quality services and programs. Trained specialists offer information about local options and develop customized lists based on each family's specific needs. Financial assistance strategies and referrals to other community resources are also available.
Learn More About R&R
Service Application
The EFSMOD Service Application offers access to essential programs for eligible families, helping them achieve their goals and ensuring successful outcomes. Services vary based on individual needs and range from direct assistance to comprehensive support programs.
Apply for Services
Program Enrollment
The EFSMOD Program Enrollment provides access to specialized programs designed to support families. These programs prepare participants for success through comprehensive services and support. Parents can choose from various program options tailored to their family's specific needs and circumstances.
